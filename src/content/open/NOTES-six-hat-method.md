# The Six-Hat evaluation method (and why it's published, not hidden)

How this work conceives and tests artefacts, proposals, and applications (a "DNA-economy cell", a coordination artefact, a candidate move). It is the disciplined, non-firehose alternative to an autonomous adversarial loop — and, because this is a Living Epistemic Work, **the method and every run are published in full.** The verdict is never handed down as a conclusion; the working is shown, including where the case was wounded and where it leapt. That is what "openness" means here: the evaluation apparatus is as transparent as the claims it evaluates (siblings: `/objections`, `/attack`).

## Why this and not a loop

An autonomous adversarial loop optimises for *survives-the-attack*, not for *truer / more alive / in-voice*. Unsupervised it over-hedges (death by a thousand concessions), entrenches the frame, and drifts from voice. And it firehoses — volume defeats assessment. The Six Hats (de Bono) fix both: they sequence generation and critique so neither dominates, and **Blue is the selector seat** — so the human audits a small, structured synthesis, not a pile. (Full reasoning: see the memory thread on recursive self-improvement.)

## The fitness function

Every hat measures against the same standard: Fuller's mission as CE's viable objective — **prefer configurations that bring greater life to all and less to none, without ecological offence.** No-regret over moves, not maximise-a-number. Each candidate is gated by the CE critics: displaced-costs, substitution-limits / binding-constraint, asymmetry. CE is generative and possibility-sized — we want *directions of movement and best-next-actions*, not legal certainty.

## The sequence (de Bono ordering, yellow before black)

- **Blue (scene)** — frame the question, the fitness function, what each hat should focus on.
- **White** — facts and data only; anchor or correct the proposal; confidence flagged on every figure; web-checked; never invented.
- **Yellow** — the disciplined optimistic case. Is it even worth doing? Lead with possibility (yellow before black, so a real idea isn't strangled in the cradle).
- **Black** — failure modes; attack *this specific* yellow case (not generic pessimism); rank by how badly each wounds it; name which CE gate fails.
- **Green** — creative ways through; take the *specific* black wounds and leap to better configurations; antifragility done right (attack absorbed, then leap). Name walls that genuinely don't yield in-horizon.
- **Red** — the factors logic can't price (tech-rate, behaviour, momentum, "people being dicks", tailwinds). A *briefing for a gut*, not a verdict.
- **Blue (close)** — reconvene, sequence the best-next-actions, name the binding constraint, state the CE-honest verdict.

## The orchestration (how the swarm runs)

A **Workflow** (deterministic): each substantive hat is a small swarm (3 agents competing *truest-in-mode* — insight, not intensity; caricature is discarded), then a Blue conductor synthesises the swarm into one hat-output before the next hat sees it. State threads forward via an accumulating dossier, so Black attacks *this* Yellow and Green solves *these* Black wounds — a relay, not six disconnected runs.

## The two human seats

**Red (the gut) and the final Blue (the verdict) stay human.** The agents do the legwork — facts, the case, the wounds, the leaps, the non-logical briefing — and the human makes the call. This is the line we hold between what is delegable (White/Yellow/Black/Green, and Red-as-briefing) and what is irreducibly the author's (the gut, and the decision). It is also how the firehose is tamed: the human reads a structured six-part synthesis and wears two hats, not a pile.

## Worked example

`NOTES-ogwen-six-hats.md` — the first full run, on the Ogwen DNA-economy cell. It is the proof the method produces decision-grade output: White corrected the proposal against real data, Black found a genuine fracture (the upregulation engine fails arithmetically at Turn 1→2), and Green leapt to a re-sequencing that survives the wounds (break "N funds N+1"; the cell owns the pattern, legibility and aggregated demand, external balance sheets own the atoms; the network is the capstone, not the leap of faith; sell the pattern).
